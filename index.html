About

What is it?

It's a backup solution. It was develop to make easy the management of our backups

ElkarBackup is a free open-source software and it's based, indeed, in other open source technologies:

  • RSnapshot
  • RSync
  • PHP
  • Symfony

Who is behind?

This project is promoted by Tknika and IMH, and it was developed by Binovo
